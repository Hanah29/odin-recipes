Dorowat

Description

Doro wat, an Ethiopian chicken stew, is a flavorful dish
featuring chicken cooked in a rich, spiced sauce made with onions,
berbere (a spice blend), and niter kibbeh (spiced clarified butter),
often served with hard-boiled eggs and injera (a flatbread).

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Niter Kibbeh
  • Berbere
  • Hard-boiled Eggs
  • injera
